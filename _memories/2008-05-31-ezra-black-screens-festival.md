The Festival Black Screens is opening its doors today in Yaounde (Cameroon). The movie to be played will be Ezra, made by Newton Aduaka. Ezra follows the story of children soldiers during the Civil war in Sierra Leone. The trailer announces an intense movie, where action, love and drama tie all together.
